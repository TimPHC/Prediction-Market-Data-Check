üìù Data Methodology Notes

  • Volume Definition: Kalshi's "volume" = Notional Volume = Number of contracts traded. Each contract has $1 notional value.
  • No Double Counting: Unlike Polymarket, Kalshi counts YES/NO as two sides of the same contract (not separate instruments). One matched trade = 1 contract.
  • Fee Structure: $0.02/contract = $0.01 (Commission to HOOD) + $0.01 (Exchange Fee to Kalshi)
